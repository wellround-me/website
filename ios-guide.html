iOS Detailed Guide

Follow these steps to seamlessly install and set up Wellround on your iOS device.

1 Installation

Install TestFlight

First, install the TestFlight app from the App Store. This is Apple's official platform for testing beta apps.

2 Invitation

Open Your Invite

Check your email for an invitation from TestFlight. Open it and tap "View in TestFlight" to accept the beta invitation for Wellround.

3 Installation

Install Wellround

Inside TestFlight, you'll see the Wellround app. Tap "INSTALL" to download and install it on your device.

4 Authentication

Login to Your Account

Open Wellround. If it's your first time, reset your password using your university email. Then, log in with your new credentials.

5 Permissions

Connect to Health

To get personalized insights, grant Wellround access to your Apple Health data. Tap "Turn On All Categories" for the best experience.

6 Configuration

Select Your Watch

Go to the settings page and choose your primary smartwatch (e.g., Apple Watch) to ensure accurate data synchronization.

You're All Set!

Congratulations! You've successfully set up Wellround on your iOS device. Explore the app, check your daily insights, and start your journey to a more balanced life. If you have any questions, visit our contact page.
