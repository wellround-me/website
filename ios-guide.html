Detailed Setup Instructions

Here you can find more detailed instructions and images for each step of the setup process.

0

Download from Test Flight

Download test flight on your iphone. Accept the invitation you received via email and an app should appear on the Test Flight App. Install it and you are ready to go!

1

Reset password

If this is your first access. Please click on forget password. You will receive an email to reset the password. Check in the spam folder!

2

Give health permissions

Once you have your new password, go to settings and give access to health settings as shown in the figures

3

Add your smartwatch

If you have a smartwatch connected to your phone, and you successfully gave the permissions youo will see a a list of smartwatches in the settings. Select the smartwatch that you want to use with the app. You CANNOT use multiple watches for now.

4

Chat with your coach

By clicking on the robot figure you will enter your personal chat. Here you can ask questions about your data and general wellness advices. Check the settings to change the personality of the LLM ü§´
