Incompatible Devices

Whoop

Whoop maintains a closed ecosystem that doesn't allow third-party applications to access health data directly. While Whoop provides excellent recovery and strain insights, the data cannot be integrated with Wellround.

Alternative: If you primarily use Whoop, consider pairing it with a compatible smartwatch for Wellround integration.
