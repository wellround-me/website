  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for reset instructions
  4. Follow the link to create a new password

    Check these common issues:

    • Look in your spam/junk folder
    • Ensure you used the correct email in the form
    • Wait 48 hours - our team reviews applications manually
    • Contact us if still missing after 48 hours
    üì± App won't install or crashes?

    • Update your operating system
    • Contact support with your device model

    • Contact support for persistent issues

    • Force close and reopen the app
