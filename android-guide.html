Once you have your new password, go to settings and give access to Health Connect (if you don't have the app download it here) and give the data permissions as shown in the figures. This allows Wellround to track your wellness data.

If you have a smartwatch connected to your phone, and you successfully gave the permissions you will see a list of smartwatches in the settings. Select the smartwatch that you want to use with the app. You CANNOT use multiple watches for now.
