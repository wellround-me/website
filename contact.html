Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we typically respond much faster.

Can I schedule a demo or consultation?

Yes! For business inquiries or enterprise features, we'd be happy to schedule a personalized demo. Contact us at business@wellround.me.

Where are you located?

Wellround is a remote-first company with team members across different time zones. We serve users globally.

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide support primarily through email and our in-app chat. This allows us to provide detailed, thoughtful responses and maintain a record of our conversations.
